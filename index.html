FAQs

What 2024 Ford Mustang® model has the most horsepower? +

The 2024 Mustang® Dark Horse™ coupe has the most horsepower. It’s powered by a modified, naturally aspirated Gen-4 5.0L Coyote™ V8 engine kicking out 500 horsepower68,* and 418 lb.-ft. of torque.68,*

*Horsepower and torque are independent attributes and may not be achieved simultaneously.

What trims are available for 2024 Ford Mustang® fastback and convertibles?+

The 2024 Ford Mustang® lineup features several standout trims: EcoBoost® Fastback, EcoBoost® Convertible, EcoBoost® Premium Fastback, EcoBoost® Premium Convertible, GT Fastback, GT Premium Fastback, GT Premium Convertible, Mustang® Dark Horse™, and Mustang® Dark Horse™ Premium.

What engines are available in the 2024 Ford Mustang® lineup?+

Ford Mustang® EcoBoost® models feature a 2.3L EcoBoost® engine, Ford Mustang® GT models feature a 5.0L Ti-VCT V8 engine and Ford Mustang® Dark Horse™ models feature a modified, Gen-4 5.0L Ti-VCT V8 engine.

What is the most fuel-efficient 2024 Ford Mustang® model?+

The 2024 Mustang® EcoBoost® coupe with the standard 2.3L EcoBoost® engine and 10-speed automatic transmission has an EPA-estimated rating of 22 city/33 hwy/26 combined mpg. *

*EPA-estimated ratings. Actual mileage will vary.
